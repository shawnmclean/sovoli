## Installing WhatsApp Business

WhatsApp Business is a separate app from regular WhatsApp. You need to download it from the app store.

<DeviceTabs
  androidContent={
    <>
      <ol>
        <li>Open the Google Play Store app on your phone</li>
        <li>Tap the search bar at the top</li>
        <li>Type "WhatsApp Business" and search</li>
        <li>Look for the app by "WhatsApp LLC" (it should show a green checkmark)</li>
        <li>Tap "Install"</li>
        <li>Wait for the app to download and install</li>
        <li>Tap "Open" when it's done</li>
      </ol>

      {/* Screenshot here: Google Play Store search results showing WhatsApp Business */}
    </>
  }
  iosContent={
    <>
      <ol>
        <li>Open the App Store app on your phone</li>
        <li>Tap the search icon at the bottom</li>
        <li>Type "WhatsApp Business" and search</li>
        <li>Look for the app by "WhatsApp LLC"</li>
        <li>Tap the download button (cloud icon or "Get")</li>
        <li>You may need to enter your Apple ID password or use Face ID</li>
        <li>Wait for the app to download and install</li>
        <li>Tap "Open" when it's done</li>
      </ol>

      {/* Screenshot here: App Store search results showing WhatsApp Business */}
    </>
  }
/>
